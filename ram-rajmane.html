About

As the Event Head of UDBHAV, Ram brings creativity, energy, and flawless execution to every program the club organizes. With a sharp sense of planning and excellent coordination skills, Ram ensures that each event is not only seamless but also leaves a lasting impact on participants and audiences alike.

Under his leadership, UDBHAV’s events will grow more engaging, dynamic, and well-structured, reflecting his passion for innovation and attention to detail. Known for his problem-solving ability and team-oriented approach, Ram inspires confidence among members and motivates them to deliver their best. His dedication to excellence makes him an integral force in shaping UDBHAV’s reputation as DYPCOE’s premier anchoring club.
