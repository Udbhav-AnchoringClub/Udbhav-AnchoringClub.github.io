Bhagyashree Tingare

Faculty Co-Ordinator
UDBHAV - Anchoring Club, DYPCOE
About

As the guiding force of Udbhav – The Official Anchoring Club of DY Patil College of Engineering, Akurdi, Dr. Bhagyashree Tingare plays a vital role in shaping the club’s vision and purpose. With her constant encouragement and mentorship, she helps the team nurture communication, leadership, and anchoring skills.

Through her support, the members gain the confidence to think creatively, stay disciplined, and deliver every event with professionalism. Her guidance continues to inspire Udbhav’s journey of growth and excellence.
